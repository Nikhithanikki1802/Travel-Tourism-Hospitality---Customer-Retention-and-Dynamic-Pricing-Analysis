**Travel, Tourism & Hospitality – Customer Retention & Dynamic Pricing Analysis**

**Project Overview**

In the highly competitive travel and hospitality industry, revenue loss often occurs due to unoptimized pricing strategies and high booking cancellations.

**This project focuses on:**

Understanding customer cancellation behavior (churn)

Analyzing seasonal demand trends

Building a data foundation for dynamic pricing systems

Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and baseline machine learning models, this project aims to deliver actionable insights for improving revenue and customer retention.

**Business Objectives**

**Primary Goals:**

Reduce booking cancellation rate

Optimize Revenue Per Available Room (RevPAR)

Enable data-driven dynamic pricing strategies

**Key Deliverables:**

Identify drivers of cancellations

Analyze pricing elasticity

Segment customers based on booking behavior

Build a seasonal pricing dashboard

**User Personas**

**1. Revenue Manager**

Optimize pricing based on demand trends

Identify peak vs low-demand periods

Adjust room rates dynamically

**2. Marketing Director**

Identify high-risk customers (likely to cancel)

Run targeted retention campaigns

Optimize promotional spending

**3. Data Scientist**

Use cleaned datasets for advanced modeling

Build scalable AI-driven pricing and churn systems

**Key Features**

**Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

Univariate & bivariate analysis

Booking trends and distributions

**Customer Segmentation**

Corporate vs Leisure travelers

Weekly/monthly pricing trends

Market-based pricing insights
